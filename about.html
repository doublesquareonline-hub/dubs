WHERE YOUNG

ATHLETES BECOME STARS

At Dublin United Basketball Stars (DUBS), we believe basketball is more than a game—
it's a powerful vehicle to shape character, build confidence, and
teach life lessons that last long after the final buzzer.

OUR STORY

Founded on the belief that basketball is more than a game, DUBS started as a small group of dedicated coaches and players in Dublin. We've grown into one of the region's premier youth basketball programs, building not just athletes, but confident, resilient young leaders.

We measure success not just by wins and championships, but by the confidence our athletes gain, the friendships they build, and the character they show on and off the court.

More than a basketball program, DUBS is a community where passion for the game transforms young players into confident, resilient leaders.

OUR MISSION

To inspire our young athletes to achieve their personal best—on and off the court.

Through expert coaching, positive reinforcement, and a focus on respect and hard work, we shape players into skilled athletes and strong, responsible individuals.

OUR VISION

To build a community where every athlete reaches their full potential.

We aim to develop skilled players who compete with heart and integrity—on the court and in life—supported by a team of dedicated coaches and parents.
